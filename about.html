Fostering Skills

At CRIST, we create an environment where your child can discover a whole new world of knowledge, where the possibilities of imagination are endless, and where each student explores ever-broadening horizons. Here, we encourage students to creatively engage to become problem solvers, critical thinkers, and innovators.
