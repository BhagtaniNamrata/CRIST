Welcome to CRIST!


At CRIST, we ignite curiosity, inspire creativity, and foster innovation in the exciting world of robotics. As a leading institution dedicated to shaping the future of technology,we provide a dynamic learning environment where students embark on a thrilling journey into the realm of robotics. Here, students of all ages and backgrounds come together to explore, design, and build cutting-edge robotic systems. Our experienced instructors, state-of-the-art facilities and hands-on approach ensure that each student not only gains a solid foundation in robotics but also develops critical problem-solving skills and a passion for STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics).
